    Our History

    Founded in 2018, HydroCar started out of a small town on the Mississippi River. By taking run-off water from the river, we transformed the engine into a steam powered force of nature. Since then our mission has been to reduce wasted water, and we have been making strides in that area. With our breakthroughs in Formula 1, with a steam powered car, our revelations have been groundbreaking.

    Why Us?

    At HydroCar, we’re redefining the future of transportation by blending innovation, sustainability, and community impact. Our vehicles are powered by groundbreaking steam technology that transforms water into clean energy, drastically reducing emissions and conserving resources. Each HydroCar is engineered with precision, offering reliability, comfort, and cutting-edge technology across our lineup, from family-friendly all-terrain vehicles to sleek, high-performance sports cars. With every purchase, you’re making a difference—50% of our profits go directly to providing essential resources, such as clean water, to communities in need. As pioneers in Formula 1, our eco-friendly racing technology proves that sustainability and high performance can coexist, inspiring innovations across the industry. Since our founding in 2018, we’ve grown to 56 locations nationwide and are preparing for global expansion. By choosing HydroCar, you’re not just driving a car—you’re driving change, building a better world, one mile at a time.
