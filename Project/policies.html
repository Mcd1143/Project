    • Financing

    At HydroCar, we are committed to not only providing exceptional vehicles and financing options but also making a meaningful impact in the community and globally. Our financing policy reflects our dedication to helping customers while giving back to those in need.

    1. Flexible Financing Options

    We believe everyone deserves access to reliable transportation. To make that possible, we offer:

    • Customizable Payment Plans: Tailored monthly payments to fit individual budgets.
    • Low-Interest Rates: Competitive rates to ensure affordability.
    • Credit Assistance: Financing solutions for customers with diverse credit backgrounds, including first-time buyers and those rebuilding credit.
    • Deferred Payments: Grace periods available for qualified buyers to help ease the financial transition.

      • 2. Community-Focused Incentives

      HydroCar is dedicated to serving and uplifting local communities. Our initiatives include: - Charitable Contributions: A portion of every vehicle sale is donated to community programs, including local water projects, education initiatives, and family assistance funds. - Trade-In Boosts: Additional trade-in credits for customers donating proceeds to community or global water initiatives. - Rewards: Exclusive discounts for returning customers who participate in volunteer or charitable activities.

      3. Global Water Access Program

      Recognizing the vital importance of clean water, HydroCar integrates a water-access initiative into every financing agreement: - HydroCar Water Pledge: For every vehicle financed, HydroCar funds the creation or maintenance of clean water systems for communities in need. - Customer Engagement: Buyers can select from a list of water projects their purchase will support, providing a personal connection to the cause. - Annual Impact Reports: Transparency is important to us. Customers receive updates on the impact of their contributions through financing with HydroCar.

      4. Sustainability Discounts

      As advocates for environmental responsibility, we encourage customers to align with our mission by offering: - Discounts on hybrid or electric vehicles. - Financing perks for choosing eco-friendly vehicle packages, such as solar-powered accessories or water-saving car washes.

      5. Educational Support

      - Financial Literacy Workshops: Free classes for customers to learn about managing loans, budgeting, and credit improvement. - Community Outreach: Partnering with schools and non-profits to educate underserved communities on both financial planning and the importance of water conservation.

      6. Transparency and Support

      - Clear Terms: No hidden fees or surprise charges in our financing agreements. - Dedicated Support Team: Customer care representatives available to assist throughout the financing process and beyond.

      Our Promise

      At HydroCar, we’re not just selling cars; we’re driving change. Every vehicle purchased helps move us closer to a world where everyone has access to clean water and a brighter future. Thank you for partnering with us on this journey.

      Deliveries

      At HydroCar, we ensure a smooth, eco-friendly delivery experience that reflects our commitment to quality and community impact.

      Delivery Options

      - Standard Delivery: Free within 50 miles. - Extended Delivery: Available beyond 50 miles for a competitive fee. - Eco-Delivery: Delivered using sustainable methods.

      Timeline

      - Delivery within 3-7 business days after purchase. - Express delivery available within 48 hours for an additional fee.

      Inspection and Handover

      - Vehicles are inspected before delivery to meet quality standards. - Delivery specialists will review vehicle features and provide documents, including registration and warranty.

      Trade-Ins

      Trade-ins are picked up during vehicle delivery. Ensure all keys and documents are ready.

      Community Impact

      Every delivery supports HydroCar’s clean water initiatives. Customers receive a certificate of impact and a HydroCar reusable water bottle.

      HydroCar delivers more than just vehicles—we deliver positive change. Thank you for choosing us!

      Returns

      At HydroCar, your satisfaction is our priority. If you're not completely happy with your purchase, we offer a straightforward return process:

      1. Eligibility:

      - Returns must be initiated within 7 days or 500 miles, whichever comes first, from the delivery or purchase date.

      2. Condition:

      - The vehicle must be returned in its original condition, with no significant damage or modifications.

      3. Process:

      - Contact our customer service team to begin the return process. - Bring the vehicle, keys, and all original documents to the dealership.

      4. Refunds:

      - Refunds will be processed after inspection and may be subject to a restocking fee.

      5. Exclusions:

      - Custom orders, heavily modified vehicles, or those purchased "as-is" are not eligible for return.
